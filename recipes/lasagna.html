Lasagna

Description

Dish for a night of dinner and a movie.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic Tomato Sauce
  • Salt
  • Motzarella

Steps

  1. Precook ground beef untill brown
  2. Layer cheese, beef, sauce, noodle and repeat
  3. Cook for 35 mins at 375f degrees
  4. Enjoy!
