Spaghetti

Description

Never can go wrong with Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Angel Pasta
  • Meat Balls
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Salt
  • Parsley
  • Minced Garlic

Steps

  1. Boil Angel Pasta for 10 minutes
  2. Cook meat balls in pan for 20 minutes, stirring often
  3. Plate pasta topped with meatballs then covered with sauce
  4. Enjoy!
