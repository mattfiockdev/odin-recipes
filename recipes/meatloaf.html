Meatloaf

Description

A meal all in one!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Bread Crumbs
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Italian Seasoning

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in large bowl with hands
  2. Shape the mass into a rectangular shape and place in baking pan
  3. Cook at 425f degrees for 45 minutes
  4. Enjoy!
